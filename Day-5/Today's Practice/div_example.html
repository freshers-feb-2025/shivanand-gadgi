Mumbai

Mumbai, India’s financial capital, is a vibrant city known for its bustling lifestyle, iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India, and the heart of Bollywood. It blends modernity with history and offers a rich cultural experience, while also facing challenges like overcrowding and traffic.


Delhi

Delhi, the capital city of India, is a bustling metropolis that blends rich history with modern life. It’s known for iconic landmarks like the Red Fort, India Gate, and Lotus Temple, and is a hub of politics, culture, and commerce.


Banglore

Banglore, known as the "Silicon Valley of India," is a tech and innovation hub with a vibrant startup culture. The city is famous for its pleasant weather, lush green spaces, and a thriving café and pub scene.
